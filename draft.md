$$M = \left( \begin{smallmatrix}
  \vdots & \vdots &  & \vdots\\
  a_1 & a_2 & \cdots & a_n\\
  \vdots & \vdots &  & \vdots
\end{smallmatrix} \right)
\cdot
\left( \begin{smallmatrix}
  \dots & b_1 & \dots\\
  \dots & b_2 & \dots\\
   & \vdots & \\
  \dots & b_n & \dots\\
\end{smallmatrix} \right)
$$
